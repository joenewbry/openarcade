## Task Sizing Guide

### Small (S) - 1-2 days
- Bug fixes
- Small feature additions  
- Documentation updates
- Asset integration

### Medium (M) - 3-4 days  
- New system components
- Complex bug investigations
- Asset creation (simple)
- UI implementation

### Large (L) - 5-7 days
- Major system development
- Complex integrations  
- Large asset packages
- Performance optimization

### Extra Large (XL) - 7+ days
- **Warning**: Break down further
- Architecture changes
- Major refactoring
- Complex external integrations

## Communication Channels

### Daily Updates
- **Format**: Brief status in team chat
- **Content**: Yesterday/Today/Blockers

### Sprint Ceremonies  
- **Planning**: Monday 10 AM (2 hours)
- **Daily Standup**: Tuesday-Friday 9 AM (15 min)
- **Demo**: Friday 3 PM (1 hour)
- **Retrospective**: Friday 4 PM (45 min)

### Escalation
- **Blocker**: Tag @projectmanager immediately  
- **Scope Change**: Schedule planning meeting
- **Emergency**: Direct message or call
